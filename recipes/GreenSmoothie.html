Quick Green Smoothie

A quick, healthy breakfast for anyone on the go. Substitute any liquid for the milk.


Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Stemmed Kale
  • 1 Cup Skimmed Milk
  • 1 Sliced Forzen Banana
  • 1/2 Green Apple, Cut into Chunks
  • 1/2 Pear, Cut into Chunks
  • 1/4 Cup Rolled Oats
  • 1 Tablespoon Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon Flax Seed Meal
  • 1 Tablespoon Wheat Germ
  • 2 Sprigs Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Blend kale, milk, banana, apple, pear, oats, peanut butter, flax seed meal, wheat germ, and parsley in a blender until smooth.
